Workflow Metrics Legend

Queued Workflows

Shows the total number of workflows waiting to be executed. These are workflows that have been submitted but are queued for processing. DBOS automatically manages the execution queue to ensure reliable processing.

Total Workflows

Counts all workflows that have been submitted to DBOS, including completed, running, and queued workflows. This gives you an overview of the total workflow activity in your system.

Completed

Shows workflows that have successfully completed execution. DBOS ensures workflows are durable and will automatically recover from failures, so completed workflows have been reliably processed.

Failed

Shows workflows that encountered errors during execution. DBOS provides automatic retry mechanisms and durability guarantees, so failed workflows may be retried based on your configuration.
